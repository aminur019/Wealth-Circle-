About WealthCircle

Founded in 2011, WealthCircle is a member-driven investment club that combines collective intelligence with disciplined investing strategies to generate superior returns.

We believe that by bringing together diverse perspectives and sharing knowledge, our members can make better investment decisions than they could alone.
